My Background

I was born in London Ontario on November 29th, 1998. From there my family and I moved to a town called Ingersoll where I've spent the majority of my life. Ingersoll is about 30 minutes east of London. Both sides of my family are Scottish with my Dad's side coming from Glasgow and my Mom's side coming from Edinburgh.
